Issues
  • You need to open your port in order to make your stream public.
  • Opera browser is unable to show password protected streams. Use another browser.
  • UPnP is a technology that allows to automatically open your port on your router.
  • UPnP will be lock when pystream detects that your router has no UPnP support. Check compatibility list.
  • Report me any issue you have.
